Core Brand Pillars
Bound's primary messaging and value propositions.

Smooth UX, Easy Onboarding

One passkey unlocks wallets on Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana. Connect your existing wallet or create one in seconds. No seed phrases, no extensions, no friction.

Relief, empowerment. "Finally, someone built this."

Spot BTC Trading & Cross-Chain Swaps

Trade BTC at market-set rates with your signature only, settling directly on Bitcoin. Swap any major asset for BTC in seconds—atomic, onchain, no intermediaries.

Control and speed. "I can trade BTC natively without trusting a CEX."

Self-Custody, Full Control

Your keys, your passkey, your account. Unlike FTX, BlockFi, or Celsius—Bound never takes custody, never lends out your Bitcoin, never uses it as collateral.

Peace of mind. "My Bitcoin is mine, and I can use it to trade, borrow, and earn."

Fixed-Rate BTC Loans, No Liquidation

Keep your Bitcoin, access dollars. Fixed-rate, fixed-term loans with zero liquidation risk and zero margin calls. Your collateral stays on Bitcoin—never rehypothecated, never pooled.

Peace of mind, control. "Borrow against Bitcoin without fear."

RFQ-Powered DeFi

Professional solvers compete in real time to give you the best price on every trade and loan. Your rate is set by the market, not a pool. Peer-to-peer, not peer-to-pool.

Fairness and transparency. "My price is earned, not extracted."

Multi-Chain, Single Account

One account for all your onchain activity across activity, including Bitcoin. Wallets unified, frictionless experience.

Simplicity at scale. "Everything in one place, no juggling."

Faster Bitcoin

Sub-30 second settlement. Trade, borrow, and settle at the speed of Bitcoin DeFi.

Speed and modernity. "Bitcoin that moves at the speed of markets."

Secure Architecture

No pooled funds. No single contract to exploit. Atomic settlement on Bitcoin. Each transaction is peer-to-peer with zero counterparty risk.

Security and trust. "Safe by design, not by promises."

Bitcoin-Native Assets

Access stablecoins, tokens, and collectibles issued directly on Bitcoin—all tradable from your self-custodial account. Bitcoin as a platform.

Innovation and possibility. "Bitcoin is more than BTC."
